A Joint Initiative Together With 

For likeminded decision makers empowering a lasting impact on the world

"MONACO POWER OF POSITIVE IMPACT SUMMIT" is in line with the vision of H.S.H. Prince Albert II of Monaco and His Government committed to maintaining a high level of climate ambition with the objective of carbon neutrality in 2050.

“MONACO POWER OF POSITIVE IMPACT SUMMIT” aims to:

  • Bring together Monaco Ambassadors Club, Club des Résidents Etrangers de Monaco (CREM), Princess Grace Foundation USA, Monaco Yacht Club, Prince Albert II of Monaco Foundation, Monaco Private Label and Monaco Private Label Young Leaders.
  • Measure our impact on the planet and support and stimulate the initiatives of all stakeholders in order to better address environmental, social and governance issues. This ambition is essentially based on the sharing of common values.

“MONACO POWER OF POSITIVE IMPACT SUMMIT“ intends to be a new international platform for the exchange of ideas, knowledge and experience promoting environmental protection, economic development through ESG criteria and personal values.

"MONACO POWER OF POSITIVE IMPACT SUMMIT" will allow participating members of the above entities to virtually network, discuss, learn and debate on the issues of disruptive technologies of the fourth industrial revolution and the factors that will influence society over the coming decade.
