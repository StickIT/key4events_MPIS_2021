BARCLAYS Chat Room

Opening times: 15h00 to 17h00 (UTC+2)

Barclays was established in Monaco in 1922. It was the first foreign wealth management firm to open its doors in Monaco’s Golden Square and is now one of the most respected financial institutions in the Principality. From Monaco, we draw on the full skills and knowledge of Barclays Group, which traces its origins to 1690 and is renowned for financial stability, international experience and innovative services.

Barclays Private Bank Monaco partnered in 2021 with the Foundation Prince Albert II and collaborated over the 4th edition of the Monaco Ocean Week. This partnership echo’s Barclays wider commitment to safeguard ocean biodiversity and the maintenance of a stable climate with the Blue Marine Foundation as announced in July 2020 (click here) to align its entire financing portfolio to the goals of the Paris Agreement and setting the ambition to be a net zero bank by 2050.

CFM Chat Room

Opening times: 13h00 to 16h00 (UTC+2)

The leading bank in the Principality

CFM Indosuez Wealth Management operates internationally while being firmly anchored in the Principality, with 30% of its shareholders from Monaco. Based on its local roots and the support of its majority shareholder, the Crédit Agricole Group, our firm is the leading banking institution in Monaco. CFM Indosuez Wealth Management was elected the Best Bank of Monaco in 2021 for the fifth consecutive year by the international magazine Global Finance and is proud to contribute to the development of the Principality’s socioeconomic fabric.

UBP Chat Room

Opening times: 13h10 to 14h30 (UTC+2)

As a family-owned bank, UBP considers responsible investing (RI) and corporate social responsibility (CSR) to be two sides of the same commitment to creating a sustainable world. In response to the major social and environmental issues the world faces, we see it as our role, as a financial intermediary, to help channel capital towards responsible investment solutions that also offer real potential to generate financial returns. We give just as much importance to controlling our carbon footprint with our CSR strategy, while promoting sustainable development through support to community initiatives, sponsorship and patronage.
